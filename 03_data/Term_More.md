### **1\. Blanchard’s Transsexualism Typology**

*Source: Wikipedia & Wiktionary (HSTS / Autogynephilia)*

Psychologist Ray Blanchard proposed a controversial two-type model for male-to-female (MtF) individuals. It is heavily criticized by modern clinicians and the trans community for being reductive and pathologizing.

* **HSTS (Homosexual Transsexual):**  
  * **Original Definition:** MtF individuals who are exclusively attracted to men (homosexual relative to their natal sex). Blanchard characterized them as being "feminine" from childhood and transitioning to live as heterosexual women.  
  * **4chan /lgbt/ usage:** Often re-interpreted as "Heterosexual Transsexual" (relative to their gender identity) or shortened to "husstuss."  
* **Autogynephilia (AGP):**  
  * **Definition:** A term coined by Blanchard (from Greek: *auto* "self", *gyne* "woman", *philia* "love"). He defined it as "a male's paraphilic propensity to be sexually aroused by the thought or image of himself as a woman."  
  * **Theory Context:** Blanchard argued that MtF individuals who are not exclusively attracted to men (including lesbians, bisexuals, and asexuals) transition due to this "paraphilic interest" rather than a primary gender identity distress.  
* **Androphilic vs. Gynephilic:**  
  * **Androphilic:** Attracted to men/masculinity.  
  * **Gynephilic:** Attracted to women/femininity.

### **2\. 4chan /lgbt/ (/tgy/) Slang**

*Source: Wiktionary (Category: 4chan /lgbt/ slang)*

The /lgbt/ board (frequently called "tgy" or "the t-word gallery") has a unique, often self-deprecating or derogatory vernacular.

* **\-moder:** A suffix used to describe how a trans person presents in public.  
  * **Boymoder:** A trans woman who presents as a boy (often while on HRT) to avoid "clocks" or for safety.  
  * **Manmoder:** A trans woman presenting as a masculine man, often implying they feel they do not "pass."  
  * **Girlmoder:** A trans man presenting as a girl.  
* **Passoid:** A trans person who passes as cisgender perfectly or effortlessly.  
* **Brainworms:** Obsessive, often irrational thoughts about one's own appearance or "clockability" (the ability for others to tell they are trans).  
* **Pooner:** A derogatory term used on 4chan for trans men, typically accompanied by specific grotesque caricatures.  
* **Hon:** A derogatory term for a non-passing trans woman (referencing the stereotypical use of "hun/hon" by older trans women).  
* **Doomposting:** Posting hopeless or extremely negative content regarding the state of trans rights or the possibility of ever "passing."
